Welcome to CinemaBase.

Android mobile application which allows you to 
browse ratings and descriptions of current top rated movies or 
search through movie database to find ratings and description of 
favorite movie titles. Find out what friends are saying about currently 
rated movies, and give your own thoughts about movies you have watched.
